---
title: Automated Analysis of Acute Ischemic Stroke
description: PhD research on AI-assisted analysis of acute ischemic stroke using multimodal medical imaging.
date: 2025-01-01
---

Research at the University of Stavanger focused on automated analysis of acute ischemic stroke using artificial intelligence. The work explores lesion characterization, CT and MRI interpretation, image registration, and clinically meaningful features in collaboration with Stavanger University Hospital and Laerdal Medical.
