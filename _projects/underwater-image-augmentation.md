---
title: Depth Jitter
description: Depth-aware augmentation for underwater object classification, accepted at OCEANS 2025 - Great Lakes.
date: 2025-08-08
---

Depth Jitter is a depth-aware augmentation technique for underwater object classification. It simulates realistic depth variations to improve robustness against depth-dependent color and visibility changes in underwater imagery.

Paper: [Depth Jitter: Seeing through the Depth](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.06227)

Code: [mim-team/Depth-Jitter](https://github.com/mim-team/Depth-Jitter)
