Authors: Md Sazidur Rahman, Kjersti Engan, Kathinka Dæhli Kurz, and Mahdieh Khanmohammadi.

This work proposes a bi-temporal framework for analyzing ischemic tissue evolution from admission CT perfusion and follow-up DWI MRI, using statistical descriptors, radiomic texture features, and deep feature embeddings.
